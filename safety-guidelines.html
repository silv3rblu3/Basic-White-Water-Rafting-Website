Safety Guidelines

1. Wear the Right Gear

Make sure you're properly equipped before hitting the rapids:

  • Wear a well-fitting life jacket.
  • Securely fasten your helmet.
  • Dress in quick-drying and comfortable clothing.
  • Wear water shoes or sandals with straps.

2. Listen to Your Guide

Our experienced guides are here to ensure your safety. Pay attention to their instructions and follow them at all times.

3. Communicate Effectively

Communication is key during white water rafting. Use the following signals to communicate with your team:

  • Hold up a flat hand to signal "stop."
  • Paddle forward when your guide gives the "forward paddle" command.
  • Paddle backward when your guide gives the "back paddle" command.
  • Lean in towards the center of the raft during sharp turns.
  • Listen for your guide's commands and respond promptly.

4. Maintain Proper Posture

Keep these tips in mind to maintain balance and stability:

  • Sit with your feet forward and knees bent.
  • Keep your center of gravity low and centered in the raft.
  • Avoid standing or shifting weight unnecessarily.

5. Be Aware of River Hazards

Stay alert and watch out for potential hazards on the river:

  • Keep an eye out for rocks, branches, and other obstacles.
  • Avoid strong currents and whirlpools.
  • Stay clear of low-hanging branches or overhanging rocks.
  • Be cautious of changing water levels and rapids.

6. Stay Hydrated and Protected

Take care of yourself during your rafting adventure:

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Apply sunscreen before getting on the water.
  • Wear sunglasses and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.

7. Stay Together as a Team

Rafting is a team sport. Stick together and work as a cohesive unit:

  • Paddle in sync with your team.
  • Support and help your teammates if they fall in the water.
  • Stay in your raft and avoid swimming in rough waters.

8. Enjoy the Adventure!

While safety is paramount, remember to have fun and enjoy the exhilarating experience of white water rafting. Embrace the thrill and make unforgettable memories!
