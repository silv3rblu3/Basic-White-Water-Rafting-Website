Rental Terms

Booking

To rent equipment, a reservation must be made in advance. Walk-in rentals are subject to availability.

Security Deposit

A security deposit is required for each rented item and will be refunded upon return of the equipment in its original condition.

Payment

Full payment for the rental is required at the time of booking.

Cancellation

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled rental date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the rental date are non-refundable.

Responsibility

Renters are fully responsible for any damage, loss, or theft of rented equipment during the rental period. Repair or replacement costs will be charged accordingly.

Late Returns

A late fee may be charged for equipment returned after the agreed-upon return time. Late fees are determined on a case-by-case basis.

Usage

The rented equipment is to be used solely for its intended purpose and in a safe and responsible manner.

Condition

Rented equipment must be returned in the same condition as when it was rented. Any excessive dirt, damage, or misuse may result in additional cleaning or repair fees.

Liability Waiver

All renters are required to sign a liability waiver before renting equipment, acknowledging the inherent risks associated with white water rafting and assuming full responsibility for their own safety.

Age Restrictions

Renters must meet the minimum age requirement specified for each rental item. Minors must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

Proper Use

Renters are expected to use the equipment responsibly, follow all safety guidelines, and abide by the instructions provided by the rental staff.

Insurance

We recommend renters have their own personal insurance coverage to protect against any personal injury or damage.

Refunds

No refunds will be provided for early returns or unused rental days.

Weather Conditions

In the event of inclement weather or unsafe river conditions, rentals may be rescheduled or refunded at the discretion of the rental facility.

Modification

These rental terms and conditions are subject to change without prior notice. The most current version will be provided at the time of rental.
